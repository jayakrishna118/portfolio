DATA ANALYST

As the name suggests, a Power BI data analyst uses Microsoft’s Power BI, to collect and synthesize data to gain information and assist in decision-making in a company. This position requires a Power BI expert, and experience with similar software would be a plus to recruiters as well.

As with any other analyst, a recruiter would like to see a bachelor’s degree in mathematics, statistics, or a similar field. But keep in mind that your experience using Power BI is what recruiters will be looking at most. So if you have any Power BI certification, make sure to highlight that.

Projects

The IoT-based Smart Baby Monitoring System uses internet-connected sensors to collect real-time data about a baby’s environment, including movement, temperature, and sound. These sensors are strategically placed in the baby’s crib or surrounding area to ensure comprehensive monitoring. Alerts are sent to parents' devices, allowing them to respond quickly to unusual activities or conditions.

This system enhances child safety and offers convenience by enabling parents to monitor their baby remotely. It leverages IoT and cloud technologies for efficient data processing, storage, and notification delivery. By providing real-time insights and alerts, it offers peace of mind to parents, particularly those with busy schedules or working outside the home.

Capstone Project

The Groundwater Level Analysis in Telangana project focuses on understanding water availability trends in the region. Data sets were collected from online sources and cleaned and modeled for meaningful insights. Using Microsoft Power BI, interactive dashboards were created to visualize the findings effectively.

The project highlights critical water resource management issues by identifying groundwater level patterns. This analysis aids in planning sustainable usage and addressing water scarcity concerns in Telangana. Through advanced data visualization, the project facilitates decision-making for policymakers and stakeholders in managing groundwater resources.
